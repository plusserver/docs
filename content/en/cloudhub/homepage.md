## Homepage

The [homepage](https://cloudhub.plusserver.com/home) is your central entry point to the customer portal and offers direct access to ordering options, administrative functions and important information about our products and services.

### Welcome area

![Welcome message](../img/homepage/welcome-message.png)

A welcome message greets you at the top of the page.
From here, you can directly access the following core functions:

- [Order products](../cloud-services)
- [Manage products already ordered](../products/product-dashboard)

In the top right-hand corner, there is also a button that takes you directly to the [service status page](https://status.plusserver.com/).

### Product overview (top 3 products)

![Product overview – top 3 products](../img/homepage/product-overview-top-3.png)

Below the welcome section, three of our most important products are prominently displayed.
Each product includes:

- a brief description
- an order button
- links to data sheets and documentation

This section provides a quick overview of key offerings.

### Accordion: Additional information & support

The next section contains three expandable areas:

#### More plusserver products

![More plusserver products](../img/homepage/accordion-more-plusserver-products.png)

After expanding, product cards are displayed.
Each card contains:

- A brief description of the product
- A link to the respective order form

This gives you a complete overview of the entire product portfolio.

#### First steps

This area helps new customers get started in the best possible way.
There are three tabs available:

##### Which cloud service should I choose?

![First steps – Cloud service](../img/homepage/first-steps-cloud-service.png)

- Three cards that lead to the following resources:
  - Comparison table for **cloud services**
  - Product catalogue **container solutions**
  - Product catalogue **databases**

##### Storage and Security

![First Steps - Storage & Security](../img/homepage/first-steps-storage-security.png)

- Information on:
  - **Storage & Backup**
  - **Cloud Security**

##### plusserver Insights

![First Steps - Insights](../img/homepage/first-steps-insights.png)

- Three cards with links to:
  - Our blog
  - An information article entitled "Why plusserver?"
  - Information about our data centres

#### Help area

![Help area](../img/homepage/help-area.png)

This area offers direct access to support.
It contains cards with buttons to the following pages:

- [Create ticket](../help/support/create-ticket)
- [Ticket overview](../help/support/ticket-overview)
- [FAQ](../help/faq)

In addition, contact details for **sales advice** are displayed:

- Link to [online appointment booking](https://get.plusserver.com/meetings/generally/beratungstermin)
- **Telephone number** (+49 (0) 8282 8550)
- **Email address** (beratung@plusserver.com)

Availability: *weekdays 09:00 – 17:00*
